********************************************************************************
Eckert I
********************************************************************************

+---------------------+----------------------------------------------------------+
| **Classification**  | Pseudocylindrical                                        |
+---------------------+----------------------------------------------------------+
| **Available forms** | Forward and inverse, spherical projection                |
+---------------------+----------------------------------------------------------+
| **Defined area**    | Global                                                   |
+---------------------+----------------------------------------------------------+
| **Alias**           | eck1                                                     |
+---------------------+----------------------------------------------------------+
| **Domain**          | 2D                                                       |
+---------------------+----------------------------------------------------------+
| **Input type**      | Geodetic coordinates                                     |
+---------------------+----------------------------------------------------------+
| **Output type**     | Projected coordinates                                    |
+---------------------+----------------------------------------------------------+


.. figure:: ./images/eck1.png
   :width: 500 px
   :align: center
   :alt:   Eckert I

   proj-string: ``+proj=eck1``
