| **Defined area**    | Global                                                   |
+---------------------+----------------------------------------------------------+
| **Alias**           | aea                                                      |
+---------------------+----------------------------------------------------------+
| **Domain**          | 2D                                                       |
+---------------------+----------------------------------------------------------+
| **Input type**      | Geodetic coordinates                                     |
